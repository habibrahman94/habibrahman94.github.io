I am a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Information Technology and Sciences (UITS), Programming Contest Mentor at UITS Computer Club, and member of Syntax and Semantic Research Group (SySeReG). Prior to joining UITS, I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering in 2017 from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET), Rajshahi, Bangladesh.

My research interests lie in the area of Software Engineering & Intelligent Systems, Natural Language Processing, Sentiment Analysis which includes Empirical Software Engineering, Mining Software Repositories, Code review process. Apart from that, I am interested in Data Mining, Machine Learning, Data Science, Big Data Analysis, Algorithms, and Systems Security.
